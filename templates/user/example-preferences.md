User prefers concise, direct responses without preamble or filler words. Responses should lead with the answer or action, not the reasoning. Avoid restating what the user said.

**Why:** User finds verbose responses slow to parse and prefers to focus on action over explanation.

**How to apply:** Keep all responses short. Skip phrases like "Great question!", "Certainly!", or trailing summaries like "In summary, I...". Go straight to the point.
